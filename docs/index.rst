PLACES CONUS
============

This is the documentation of data and methods used to develop estimates of the **fair market value of land** for vacant parcels in the contiguous United States (CONUS).

The research was funded by the National Science Foundation's (NSF) Human-Environment and Geographical Sciences (HEGS) program (award `#2149243 <https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2149243>`_).

PLACES, the "Private-Land Conservation Evidence System", is a parcel-level data synthesis platform developed to facilitate conservation planning at large scales and high resolutions through research into anticipated ecological, economic, and social effects of land policy and conservation interventions in the contiguous United States (CONUS).
